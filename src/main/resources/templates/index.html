About us

We are passionate breeders and caretakers who built MyRabbitry to simplify the daily care and record-keeping of rabbits. Our team consists of experienced farmers and qualified programmers who believe that all animals desrve humane care.

Our mission

To help rabbitries of all sizes stay organized, humane, and thriving by providing simple tools that respect your time and your animals.
